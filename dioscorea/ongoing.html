Ongoing Research

The following are the broad categories of research currently being undertaken towards enhanced yam cultivation and food security:

  • Biodiversity
  • Breeding Efficiency
  • Clean Seed Propagation
  • Economics
  • Sustainability and Crop Management
  • Micropropagation for higher breeding efficiency
  • Macropropagation
  • Pests & Diseases
  • Shelf-Life/Storage
  • Technology Transfer
