Dioscorea Basics

Dioscorea, the monocot which produces yams, has over 600 different species, which originate from the tropical zones of Asia and Africa depending on the species. It has been cultivated for more than 2,000 years, now feeding more than 300M people in Agrica, but still has enormous challenges in propagation to feed a still hungry and growing population. Some varieties of yams are used medicinally.
