Common Names

Commonly referred to as yams—not to be confused with the dicot, sweet potato—Dioscorea, with its 600+ species, goes by many names. Those that are cultivated for food consumption are collectively referred to as 'sain'. Courtesy of the book, Heidemarie Vos' Passion of a Foodie - An Internation Kitchen Companion, the following are a list of species and their associated common names:

Dioscorea aculeata Guinea yam, Kaawi, Afou
Dioscorea alata Barbadoes Yam, Greater yam, Indian yam, Malay yam, Winged-stalked yam, 10 months yam
Dioscorea atropurpurea dark purple yam, Malacca
Dioscorea batatas Indian potato, Chinese yam
Dioscorea bulbifera Climbing air potato, potato yam, otaheite potato
Dioscorea cayensis Guinea yam, yellow yam, 12 month yam
Dioscorea fargesii Upeh Chinese yam
Dioscorea globosa Globe yam
Dioscorea japonica Japanese yam, yamanoimo
Dioscorea rotundata negro yam, white yam, 8 months yam
Dioscorea sativa common yam
Dioscorea trifida Cush-cush yam, Yampi yam
Dioscorea upesi Chinese yam
Dioscorea villosa wild yam
